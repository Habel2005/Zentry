OFFICIAL ZENTRY COLLEGE DATA (TIST - Toc H Institute of Science and Technology)

### GOLDEN SUMMARY: COURSES & BRANCHES (B.TECH)
Zentry College (TIST) offers the following B.Tech (Undergraduate) programs.
**Duration:** 4 Years (8 Semesters).
**Eligibility:** Pass in Higher Secondary Examination with 45% marks in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.

**List of B.Tech Programs & Intake:**
1. Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) - Intake: 180 seats. NBA Accredited.
2. Electronics & Communication (ECE) - Intake: 60 seats. NBA Accredited.
3. Mechanical Engineering (ME) - Intake: 60 seats. NBA Accredited.
4. Civil Engineering (CE) - Intake: 30 seats. NBA Accredited.
5. Safety & Fire Engineering (SFE) - Intake: 60 seats. NBA Accredited.
6. Information Technology (IT) - Intake: 60 seats.
7. Electrical & Electronics (EEE) - Intake: 30 seats.
8. Robotics & Automation - Intake: 60 seats.
9. Electrical & Computer Engineering - Intake: 30 seats.                          

### GOLDEN SUMMARY: POSTGRADUATE PROGRAMS
**MBA Program:** Full-time, 2 Years (4 Semesters). Specializations in Finance, Marketing, HR, Operations.
**M.Tech Program:** 2 Years. Offered in 7 specializations including Data Security and VLSI.

We also offer Postgraduate (M.Tech) programs in 7 specializations and a full-time MBA program (2 years) with specializations in Finance, Marketing, HR, Operations, and Information Systems.

### GOLDEN SUMMARY: ADMISSION & ELIGIBILITY
- Merit Quota (50% of seats): allotted by CEE Kerala based on KEAM Rank. Requires 45% aggregate marks in PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Maths) in Class 12.
- Management Quota (35% of seats): Allotted by the Institute based on Class 12 PCM marks (Minimum 45%) and a valid KEAM or JEE score.
- NRI Quota (15% of seats): Allotted by Institute based on Class 12 PCM marks (Minimum 45%). KEAM/JEE is NOT mandatory.

### LATERAL ENTRY ADMISSION
Lateral Entry is available for Diploma holders.
- Merit Quota (Lateral): Allotted by LBS based on Diploma marks and LET (Lateral Entry Test) rank.
- Management Quota (Lateral): Based on Diploma marks and LET rank.
Intake for Lateral Entry: Computer Science (18), Civil (3), Mechanical (6), Safety (3), EEE (3), EC (6), IT (6), Robotics (6).

### HOSTEL FACILITIES
TIST provides separate in-campus hostels for boys and girls with a total capacity of 700 inmates.
Facilities: Well-furnished rooms, vegetarian and non-vegetarian mess, Wi-Fi, study rooms, recreation areas, and 24x7 security.
Hostels are available for both UG and PG students.
Admission Requirement: Hostel application form and affidavit are mandatory.

### ACCREDITATION & RECOGNITION
NAAC Accreditation: TIST is NAAC accredited and was the only college under CUSAT to receive this at the time.
NBA Accreditation: Valid until June 30, 2026, for five programs: Civil, Computer Science, Electronics & Communication, Mechanical, and Safety & Fire Engineering.
This accreditation allows students to pursue B.Tech (Honours).

### HISTORY & LEGACY
Established: 2002.
Roots: Toc H International was founded during WWI by Rev. Tubby Clayton in Poperinge, Belgium.
Meaning: "Toc H" is the signaler's code for "TH" (Talbot House).
Toc H India: Started in Kochi in 1972, initially focusing on humanitarian service for leprosy and TB patients before entering education.
